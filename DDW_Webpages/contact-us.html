Frequently Asked Questions

A standing desk is a desk that allows you to stand up comfortably while working. Many modern versions are adjustable so you can change the height and alternate between sitting and standing.

Using a standing desk can help reduce back pain, improve mood and energy levels, and may even boost productivity. It can also help lower the risk of weight gain and heart disease.

When choosing a standing desk, consider factors such as adjustability, stability, ease of use, and price. It's also important to ensure the desk fits your workspace and supports your work habits.
